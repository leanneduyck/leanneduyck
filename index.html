a bit about me. . .

As a music educator turned WordPress Web Designer, I use structured creativity when building intuitive, user-friendly websites. My experience in teaching has shaped my skills in communication and content organization, enabling me to design digital spaces that are both engaging and easy to navigate.

I am passionate about crafting responsive web designs that adapt seamlessly across devices. Comfortable in HTML, CSS, Javascript, and WordPress, I specialize in creating websites that don't just look good - they work effortlessly for diverse users.

Armed with a B.A. in Music Education from Seattle Pacific University, a Full-Stack Web Development certificate from CareerFoundry,
and a deep enthusiasm for all things WordPress, I am excited to collaborate on projects that blend creativity with strategy!
