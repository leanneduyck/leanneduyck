About

As a music educator turned web developer, I bring a unique perspective to any development team.

With a decade of experience as a music educator, I've developed strong skills in project management, communication, and creative problem-solving.
My transition into web development was driven by my passion for creating organized and satisfying projects, and a commitment to continuous learning.

Just as music is about learning and mastering languages of notes and rhythms, I apply the same principles to learning and mastering programming languages to create beautiful and functional apps. My background in music education has given me a strong foundation in analytical thinking, attention to detail, empathy, and the ability to meet deadlines while maintaining high standards.

I am eager to bring my skills and love of structured creativity to the tech world, where the art of coding harmonizes with the discipline of music to craft beautiful and functional apps!
