About

As a music educator turned frontend developer and technical writer, I bring a unique blend of communication, empathy, and technical expertise to the tech world. With a decade of experience in education, I’ve honed my skills in audience awareness, structured creativity, and simplifying complex concepts. My transition into technology was driven by a passion for constructing organized, user-centered applications and a commitment to continuous learning.

Just as music involves mastering the languages of notes and rhythms, I apply the same principles to learning programming languages, allowing me to build beautiful and functional applications that resonate with users. My teaching experience has instilled in me the importance of understanding specific audience needs, a principle I carry over into crafting clear and user-friendly documentation for complex tech concepts and procedures.

I combine my expertise in modern web development technologies—such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React—with strong analytical thinking and organizational skills. This unique blend enables me to effectively bridge the gap between technical and non-technical audiences, ensuring that all users can navigate and understand the products I help create.

My unique background and skill set are perfectly suited for the modern tech world, where the art of coding harmonizes with the discipline of music to create beautiful and accessible applications!
